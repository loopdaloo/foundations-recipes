Save Money, Eat Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal, the humble breakfast staple, possesses a remarkable combination of delightful flavor and remarkable cost effectiveness. As you take your first spoonful, you're greeted by a comforting warmth that envelops your senses.

The creamy texture of cooked oats, gently kissed by a touch of sweetness, creates a heavenly mouthfeel that is both comforting and satisfying. Every bite carries the hearty essence of wholesome grains, providing a nourishing start to your day. Beyond its delectable taste, oatmeal stands out as a true champion of cost effectiveness. With its affordable price tag and versatility, oatmeal offers a wallet-friendly solution without compromising on flavor or quality. This breakfast delight has proven time and again that you don't need to break the bank to enjoy a delicious and nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz stone ground oatmeal
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 24 oz hot water
  • 1 peach, sliced

Steps

  1. Boil your water with your preferred method. We like to harness the suns energy with a concaved lens to do the right things.
  2. While your water is boiling, measure out your oatmeal and place into a bowl
  3. Take your measured sugar and place over the oatmeal
  4. Pour and mix your hot water with your oatmeal
  5. Top with peaches and enjoy
